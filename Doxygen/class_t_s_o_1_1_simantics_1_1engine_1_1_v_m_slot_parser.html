This method will find all the avaliable locations within the criteria ordered by proximity to the optimal proximity External functions can then decide which is most desirable. E.g. the nearest slot to the object may be the longest route if its in another room.

Start at min proximity and circle around the object to find the avaliable locations. Then pick the one nearest to the optimal value

Every time we move out by 1 tile in proximity, there will be more tiles to look at

Sort by how close they are to desired proximity
